How to send an E-mail
=====================

In the Hierachy you will find a GameObject called "EmailManager" under the "Utils" GameObject.

In the inspector of the "EmailManager" GameObject fill in the fields under the "E-Mail" header.
These fields include:
	The email address you want to send the E-mail from and the password for that E-mail address.
	The email address you want to send the E-mail to.
	The file path of the attachment you wish to include, for example "C:\Users\USE\Desktop\attachmentFile.png"
	The mail client to use to send the E-mail, Gmail and Hotmail are already set up and ready to be used.
